Where We Shake Things Up!

At Shaky, we believe life is too short for boring desserts. We aren’t just blending milk and ice cream; we’re crafting thick, velvety, over-the-top masterpieces designed to make your tastebuds do a happy dance.

Our journey started with a simple, slightly chaotic idea: take the nostalgic comfort of a classic diner milkshake and drag it into the modern world with wild flavors, premium ingredients, and a serious lack of impulse control.

What Makes Us Tick and Mix

We get it—you can get a milkshake anywhere. But you can't get a Shaky shake anywhere else. Here is our secret recipe for success:

  1. The Real Deal Dairy: We use 100% premium, locally sourced ice cream as our canvas. No icy fillers, no cutting corners.
  2. Next-Level Mix-ins: From fresh, locally baked brownies and artisanal sauces to childhood-favorite cereals and nostalgic candies, if it tastes amazing, we’ve probably tried to blend it.
  3. The "Shaky" Twist: We love experimenting. Alongside your favorite classic vanilla and chocolate, you’ll always find a rotating menu of chaotic, limited-edition seasonal flavors.
  4. Vegan & Dairy-Free Love: We believe nobody should miss out on the fun. We offer an incredible lineup of oat- and coconut-based shakes that rival the real thing.

Whether you’re stopping by to cure a late-night craving, celebrating a win or just need a little sweet escape from the daily grind, we’ve got a blender with your name on it.

Come on in, grab a seat, and let’s get Shaky!
